# User Manual

Welcome to the Statsang Portal! This guide explains the features available to a standard User.

## 1. Dashboard
Your starting point. Here you can see:
- A quick percentage overview of your attendance.
- A summary of recent Notices.
- Upcoming Events.
- Your most recent Journal entries.

## 2. Events
View the community calendar. 
- You can read event details like date, time, and venue.
- Click **Register** to secure your spot at an event.
- If you're already registered, the card will display a "Registered" badge.

## 3. My Attendance
Track your personal presence across past gatherings.
- View a historical list of all Sabhas.
- See whether you were marked `PRESENT` or `ABSENT` by an admin.
- View precise start and end times for the sessions you attended.

## 4. My Journal
A private space for your personal reflections.
- **Create**: Click "New Entry" to log learnings from a specific date.
- **Edit/Delete**: You have full control to manage your past entries.
- *Note: Journals are private to you, though Administrators possess read-access for platform moderation.*

## 5. Gallery
A visual repository of community events.
- Browse photos and albums uploaded by the administration team.

## 6. Notices
A read-only board of community announcements.
- Important notices are flagged with a `HIGH` priority badge.

## 7. My Profile & Settings
Manage your account security and details.
- Update your **Full Name**, **Username**, and **Email**.
- Securely change your **Password**. (You will be required to log in again after a successful password change).
